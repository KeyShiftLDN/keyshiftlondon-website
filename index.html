young people don't

We know the schools, estates and communities where potential gets buried under pressure. Key Shift London is a South London Community Interest Company that builds the structure around young people aged 12–18 — so they can rise.

We equip parents, mentors, coaches, pastoral leads, schools and councils with the tools, training and technology to support real development — not one-off programmes, but infrastructure that lasts.

Chapter 02 — What We Change

we target the
whole life,
not the symptom.

The youth development landscape is fragmented — interventions are short, unmeasured, and disconnected from the environments that actually shape young people.

We address this through Youth Precision Work: a structured framework built on five core areas — the SHAPE model.

We don't just run programmes. We establish youth development infrastructure that schools, PRUs, councils and academies can build on.
